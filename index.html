academics/web3/canada

We are a group of academic researchers at Canadian universities working on web3, blockchain technologies, and cryptocurrencies. a/w/c is split into three groups: devotees, aficionados, and enthusiasts.

  • Devotees primarily work on projects related to web3. We are highly dedicated, often leading initiatives and pushing the boundaries of the field.
  • Aficionados primarily work on different topics but have made meaningful contributions to web3. We are passionate and knowledgeable, contributing valuable insights and expertise from our primary fields.
  • Enthusiasts have minimal experience with web3 but are interested in exploring it. We are curious and eager to learn, bringing fresh perspectives and energy to the field.
